Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how SeeStox handles information when you use our app.

1. Introduction

SeeStox is an AI-powered stock market insights and analytics app for users in India (18+). It provides market insights, sentiment, expected range, watchlists, news context, and an AI assistant. This policy applies to the SeeStox mobile application and the Privacy Policy page at /privacypolicy/.

By using the app, you consent to this Privacy Policy. SeeStox does not provide investment advice or guaranteed returns.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide and information generated by your use of the app:

  • Personal information: email address and name (if provided)
  • Account and authentication data: login credentials or OAuth tokens
  • Usage and analytics data: features used, pages visited, and in-app actions
  • Device and technical data: device model, OS version, app version, IP address, language, and crash logs
  • Subscription and payment status: plan type, payment references, and validity

We do not collect sensitive financial credentials such as bank account numbers, card or UPI details, brokerage login passwords, or transaction PINs.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • Maintain user sessions
  • Improve performance and reliability
  • Analyze usage trends

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

4. How We Use Information

  • Provide core app functionality and account access
  • Generate AI-based insights from market data
  • Personalize experience (watchlists, preferences)
  • Maintain security, prevent misuse, and detect fraud
  • Provide customer support and respond to requests
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

5. AI and Automated Processing

SeeStox uses AI models to analyze historical and publicly available market data and to respond to user queries. AI outputs are informational only and are not financial advice.

We do not guarantee accuracy or returns based on AI outputs.

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share limited data with trusted third parties for the following purposes:

  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Cloud infrastructure and hosting
  • Payment processing, if you purchase a subscription

We do not sell personal data and do not share data for advertising purposes.

Payment credentials are handled by payment gateways (for example, Razorpay). We receive payment references and status, but not your card or UPI details.

7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce policies.

If you request account deletion, we will delete or de-identify your data unless retention is required by law.

8. User Rights and Choices

You can request the following at any time:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your account and data
  • Withdrawal of consent where applicable

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@seestox.com.

9. Data Security

We use reasonable security practices such as access controls, secure storage, and encryption in transit where appropriate.

However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's Privacy

SeeStox is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn that a child has provided personal data, we will delete it.

11. Policy Availability

This Privacy Policy is accessible within the app, on the Google Play Store listing, and at /privacypolicy/.

12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version in the app and on /privacypolicy/. Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Information
