Account & Data Deletion - SeeStox

To request deletion of your SeeStox account and personal data, please email support@seestox.com from your registered email address.

Steps to request deletion

  1. Email support@seestox.com from your registered email.
  2. Use the subject: Delete My SeeStox Account
  3. Include your registered email and a brief request to delete your account.

Data deleted

  • Account profile
  • Email ID
  • Watchlist data
  • App preferences/settings

Data retained (if applicable)

  • Anonymous analytics (if any)
  • Legal/compliance records if required by law

Processing time

Processed within 7 working days after verification.

Cannot access your account?

Email us from any email address and include your registered email and your last login date (approx).

For more details, please review our privacy policy: https://api.seestox.com/privacypolicy/
